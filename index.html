Hi Chat is an individual-owned mobile application. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. Hi Chat can be used without providing personal details such as name, email address, or phone number.

The application may request access to the following device features strictly for functionality:

Hi Chat uses Local Storage on your device to store basic app preferences and improve user experience. No tracking cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics tools are used.

4. Third-Party Services

Hi Chat supports Google Login for authentication. Any information shared through Google Login is handled directly by Google according to their Privacy Policy. We do not store or misuse this information.

5. Advertising & Analytics

  • No advertisements are displayed.
  • No analytics or tracking software is used.
  • No Facebook Pixel or retargeting technologies are implemented.

6. Payments

Hi Chat does not process or accept online payments.

7. Children's Privacy

Hi Chat is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, shared, or misused by us.
